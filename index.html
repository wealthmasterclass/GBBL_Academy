• Overview

“Knowledge gives you POWER; whilst The Use of Knowledge makes you POWERFUL”.

The Wealth Masterclass is a short term Entrepreneurship and Financial Coaching program aimed at training and nurturing emerging entrepreneurs to become effective managers of their money while building generational wealth.

The program is designed for individuals who seek to gain knowledge and skills that are required to enable them earn more income, become adequately protected, debt-free and financially independent.

Thus the Wealth Masterclass training offers a variety of unique practical experiences that allows associates (partners) to develop and build upon critical skills set necessary to enable them to build the needed financial resource for their business initiative(s) and become effective financial coaches and advisers, thereby increasing their impact on positive client outcomes.

Associates of the Wealth Masterclass shall be required to complete ALL 6 modules of the program, to demonstrate their abilities relative to the four (4) core values of Excellence, Leadership, Teamwork and Professionalism.

Hence, while other financial programs give you "power", the Wealth Masterclass makes you "powerful".

  • Eligibility (Who can apply):
Applicants to The Wealth Masterclass must meet two sets of criteria;
  1. Be between the ages 25-49 years.
  2. Be a trained Professional (with at least an HND).

  • Program Duration:
The Wealth Masterclass is a 6-12 MONTHS entrepreneurship program.

  • Program Completion (Graduation):
Upon successful completion of the Wealth Masterclass, an associate is officially inducted as a member of the prestigious Business Opportunity Network (B.O.N.), in addition with the following as his/her graduation package:
  1. A car (worth USD 25,000)
  2. A monthly income of GHc50,000
  3. A monthly T-bill principal investment of GHc50,000
This “graduation package” is to ensure that every graduate of the entrepreneurs academy - Wealth Masterclass, is adequately positioned to establish a business in an area(s) / field(s) of their interest and offer employment opportunities to others.

  • The Wealth Masterclass Open Day:

The Wealth Masterclass Open Day is aimed at offering interested entrepreneurs the opportunity to comprehensively evaluate what the Masterclass entails as well as seek answers (clarity) to all possible queries to enable them make an informed decision relative to their application. Each Wealth Masterclass Open Day edition entails 4 weekly forums – i.e. every Saturday for 4 weeks.

NB: Participation in the Wealth Masterclass Open Day is FREE. However interested persons will have to pre-register online.
