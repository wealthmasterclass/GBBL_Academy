THE WEALTH MASTERCLASS

…raising Ghanaian Millionaires

  • Overview

“Knowledge gives you POWER; whilst The Use of Knowledge makes you POWERFUL”.

The GGBL Academy Wealth Masterclass is a short term entrepreneurship program aimed at nurturing young entrepreneurs to become millionaires.

The program is designed to provide emerging Global Business Leaders of Ghanaian descent, with a variety of unique experiences that will serve to develop and broaden critical skill sets and competencies as they progress their development towards leadership roles within the business ecosystem.

Associates of the Wealth Masterclass will be assigned to projects in relation to the core values of the academy – i.e. Professionalism, Excellence, Leadership and Teamwork.

Thus, while other business programs give you "power", the Wealth Masterclass makes you "powerful".

  • Eligibility (Who can apply):
Applicants to The Wealth Masterclass must meet two sets of criteria;
  1. Be between the ages 25-49 years.
  2. Be a trained Professional (with at least an HND).

  • Program Duration:
The Wealth Masterclass is a 6 MONTHS entrepreneurship program.

  • Program Completion (Graduation):
Upon successful completion of the Wealth Masterclass, an associate is officially inducted as a member of the prestigious Business Opportunity Network (B.O.N.), in addition with the following as his/her graduation package:
  1. A car (worth USD 24,000)
  2. A monthly income of GHc50,000
  3. A monthly T-bill principal investment of GHc50,000
This “graduation package” is to ensure that every graduate of the GGBL academy - Wealth Masterclass, is adequately positioned to establish a business in an area(s) / field(s) of their interest and offer employment opportunities to others.

  • The Wealth Masterclass Orientation:
The Wealth Masterclass orientation is COMPULSORY for ALL applicants.
The orientation window is aimed at offering interested applicants the opportunity to comprehensively evaluate what the Masterclass entails as well as seek answers (clarity) to all possible queries to enable them make an informed decision relative to their application. Each Wealth Masterclass Orientation comprises 4 weekly forums – i.e. every Saturday for 4 weeks.

NB: Applicants to the Wealth Masterclass will need to submit an Online Orientation Application and pay a non-refundable GHc50.00 orientation fee. The non-refundable orientation fee which covers for resource materials and snacks is to be paid at the venue for the Masterclass Orientation.
